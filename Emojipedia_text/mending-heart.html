Emoji Meaning

A heart shown with a bandage across one side; the opposite of a broken heart. May be used to indicate the process of healing, recovery, or to express sympathy for someone going through a difficult time.

The Mending Heart emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining ❤️ Red Heart, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🩹 Adhesive Bandage. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Mending Heart was added to Emoji 13.1 in 2020.
