Emoji Meaning

A poodle, a popular dog breed with curly hair, often groomed in an ornamental pattern.

Generally depicted as a grayish-white poodle in full profile on all fours facing left, with a shaved torso, legs, and face with fuller, pom-pom-like hair on its head, ears, chest, feet, and tip of tail (as the Continental Clip).

Like 🐕 Dog, may be used to represent a variety of dog breeds (often fluffy or long-haired). See also 🐶 Dog Face.

Google, Samsung, and Facebook’s designs previously featured pink poodles.

Poodle was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
