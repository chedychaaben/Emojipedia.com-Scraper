Emoji Meaning

A fire extinguisher, as used to put out small fires. Depicted as a red tank with a black hose and pressure gauge. Most platforms include an image of a flame on a white label.

Commonly used for various content concerning fire safety, emergencies, and literal and figurative fires.

Fire Extinguisher was approved as part of Unicode 11.0 in 2018 and added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
