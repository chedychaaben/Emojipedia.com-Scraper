Emoji Meaning

A battery with a minimal amount of power remaining, indicated by the color red. Used to show low power or energy and the need to be plugged in or recharged.

Low Battery was approved as part of Unicode 14.0 in 2021 and added to Emoji 14.0 in 2021.
