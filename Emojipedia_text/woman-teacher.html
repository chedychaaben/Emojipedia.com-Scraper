Emoji Meaning

A woman standing in front of a chalkboard (aka blackboard) with the intention of teaching a class. Often shown holding a 📕 Closed Book or a pointer.

The Woman Teacher emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👩 Woman, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🏫 School. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Woman Teacher was added to Emoji 4.0 in 2016.
