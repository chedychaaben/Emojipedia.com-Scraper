Emoji Meaning

A rock climber, shown climbing up a rock formation. Some platforms show the climber with a helmet and/or rope. The Emojipedia Sample image was created with assistance from World Champion Rock Climber Sasha DiGiulian.

Variations exist for 🧗‍♀️ Woman Climbing and 🧗‍♂️ Man Climbing.

Person Climbing was approved as part of Unicode 10.0 in 2017 and added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
