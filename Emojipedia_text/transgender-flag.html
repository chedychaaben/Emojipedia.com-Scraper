Emoji Meaning

A flag with horizontal pale blue and pale pink stripes with a single white stripe in the middle, used as a Transgender or Transgender Pride flag and commonly seen at Pride events.

The Transgender Flag emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🏳️ White Flag, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and ⚧️ Transgender Symbol. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Transgender Flag was added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
