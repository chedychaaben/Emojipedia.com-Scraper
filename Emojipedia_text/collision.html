Emoji Meaning

A cartoon-styled representation of a collision. Depicted as a star-like, orange-and-yellow fiery burst, generally with one point extending to the bottom right, as if from the source of the collision.

May be used to represent various types of bangs, explosions, or crashes. Like 🔥 Fire and 💯 Hundred Points, commonly used to represent something is excellent or exciting in some way.

Apple implements a version of 💥 Collision in its 🧨 Firecracker and 📸 Camera With Flash.

Collision was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Collision Symbol” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
