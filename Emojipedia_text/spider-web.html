Emoji Meaning

A classic spider web (cobweb), as spun by a 🕷️ Spider to catch prey. Depicted as a white, symmetrical, wheel-shaped web, as an orb web, with six or eight equal sectors.

Commonly used during Halloween, to represent the superhero Spider-Man, or for a metaphorical web, net, or network. Not to be confused with ❄️ Snowflake.

Spider Web was approved as part of Unicode 7.0 in 2014 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
