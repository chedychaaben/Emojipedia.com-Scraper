Emoji Meaning

An urn, as used to hold the cremated ashes of the dead. Appearance varies, but generally depicted as a covered, bronze container with a rounded body and tapered foot.

May be used for various content concerning death and funerals. May also be used for other types of vessels, such as pots or vases.

Not to be confused with 🏺 Amphora.

Funeral Urn was approved as part of Unicode 4.1 in 2005 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
