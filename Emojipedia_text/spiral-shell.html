Emoji Meaning

A seashell, as found at the beach. Generally depicted as an empty, spiraling, white seashell, as grown by a sea snail or home to a hermit crab. Positioned at various angles across platforms.

Often used for various content concerning beaches, ocean and ocean life, and summer fun.

Apple’s design previously resembled a clam shell, Microsoft’s a snail shell. Google’s was previously blue.

Spiral Shell was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
