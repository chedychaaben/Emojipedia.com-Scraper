Emoji Meaning

The Handshake: Medium-Light Skin Tone, Medium Skin Tone emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🫱 Rightwards Hand, 🏼 Medium-Light Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner, 🫲 Leftwards Hand and 🏽 Medium Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Handshake: Medium-Light Skin Tone, Medium Skin Tone was added to Emoji 14.0 in 2021.
