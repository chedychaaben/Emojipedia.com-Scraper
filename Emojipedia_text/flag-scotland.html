Emoji Meaning

The flag for Scotland, a country in the United Kingdom. May show as the letters gbsct.

The Flag: Scotland emoji is a tag sequence combining 🏴 Black Flag, 󠁧 Tag Latin Small Letter G, 󠁢 Tag Latin Small Letter B, 󠁳 Tag Latin Small Letter S, 󠁣 Tag Latin Small Letter C, 󠁴 Tag Latin Small Letter T and 󠁿 Cancel Tag. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Flag: Scotland was added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.
