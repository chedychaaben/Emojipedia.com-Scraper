Emoji Meaning

A person kneeling on both knees. May be used to represent someone in prayer, or a person resting on the floor.

Person Kneeling was approved as part of Unicode 12.0 in 2019 under the name “Kneeling Person” and added to Emoji 12.0 in 2019.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
