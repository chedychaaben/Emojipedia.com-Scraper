Emoji Meaning

A shrimp (prawn), a small crustacean with a long tail. Generally depicted as a reddish-orange shrimp (as cooked) in full profile facing left, with tiny eyes, many limbs, and its long antennae curling back as its long tail curls in.

May be consumed in the form of 🍤 Fried Shrimp or 🥘 Shallow Pan of Food. Often used to represent seafood.

The antennae of Apple’s shrimp curls under its body. The body of Facebook’s shrimp is straight. Google and Samsung’s shrimp previously faced right.

Shrimp was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
