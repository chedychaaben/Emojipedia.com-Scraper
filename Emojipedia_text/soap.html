Emoji Meaning

A bar of soap, as used in the shower, bath or for hand washing. Generally shown with lathered bubbles. Depicted in various colors, often pink, and at different angles.

Commonly used for various content concerning bathing, washing, and cleaning. May be used to represent the opposite of dirt or being dirty. Recommended for use to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

Soap was approved as part of Unicode 11.0 in 2018 under the name “Bar of Soap” and added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
