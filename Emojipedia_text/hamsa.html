Emoji Meaning

A blue hand with an eye in the center of the palm.

The hamsa is an ancient symbol important to multiple religions including Islam, Judaism, Buddhism, and Christianity. It represents luck, protection, guidance, feminine power, and faith, among other meanings.

Hamsa was approved as part of Unicode 14.0 in 2021 and added to Emoji 14.0 in 2021.
