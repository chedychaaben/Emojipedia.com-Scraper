Emoji Meaning

A swirl of soft-serve ice cream atop a wafer-style cone, white or cream-colored, as vanilla, on most platforms. Facebook’s design suggests strawberry. May be used more generally for ice cream, frozen yogurt, and frozen treats.

Soft Ice Cream was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
