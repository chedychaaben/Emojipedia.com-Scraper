Emoji Meaning

A bottle of lotion for the skin, such as moisturizer or sunscreen (sunblock). May be used for other hygienic liquids, including shampoo and body wash, as well as beauty products.

Appearance varies across platforms, but commonly depicted in white and gold colors. Some platforms, like Apple and Twitter, feature a bottle with a pump while others show a cylinder with a cap.

Lotion Bottle was approved as part of Unicode 11.0 in 2018 and added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
