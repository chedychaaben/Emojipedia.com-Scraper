Emoji Meaning

A hat worn by women for sun protection, or for fashion.

The Apple version of this hat is made of straw and has a green bow on the front. Google shows a straw hat with a purple ribbon, though this ribbon was previously red. Twitter's design features a flower on the front of the hat, as did an early Microsoft design.

Woman’s Hat was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Womans Hat” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
