Emoji Meaning

A person (cyclist) who rides a mountain bike: a type of off-road bicycle. Intended to be gender-neutral.

Person Mountain Biking was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Mountain Bicyclist” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
