Emoji Meaning

A plain pink heart, expected to be frequently used alongside other hearts. Used to represent love, friendship, and feelings of warmth, and the color pink.

On several platforms, pink hearts are used in the design of other emojis. These emoji include: Sparkling Heart 💖, Two Hearts 💕, Growing Heart 💗, Beating Heart 💓, Revolving Heart 💞, Heart with Arrow 💘, Heart with Ribbon 💝, and Kiss 💏.

Pink Heart was approved as part of Unicode 15.0 in 2022 and added to Emoji 15.0 in 2022.

🚩 Approved in September 2022 as part of Emoji 15.0. Now available for select Samsung devices and Google platforms, and within Apple's iOS 16.4 beta. Expected to come to other platforms throughout 2023.

Displays in the monochrome Noto Emoji design on Emojipedia if your device has yet to provide support.
