Emoji Meaning

The Deaf Man: Medium-Light Skin Tone emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧏 Deaf Person, 🏼 Medium-Light Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and ♂️ Male Sign. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Deaf Man: Medium-Light Skin Tone was added to Emoji 12.0 in 2019.
