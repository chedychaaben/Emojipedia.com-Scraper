Emoji Meaning

A yellow face winking with puckered lips blowing a kiss, depicted as a small, red heart. May represent a kiss goodbye or good night and convey feelings of love and affection more generally. 

Many platforms feature the same eyes as on their 😉 Winking Face.

Microsoft’s design previously featured a feminine smiley with long eyelashes and red lipstick, which could leave a kiss mark.

Face Blowing a Kiss was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Face Throwing a Kiss” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
