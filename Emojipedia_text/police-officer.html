Emoji Meaning

A police officer wearing a blue hat with a gold badge or shield in the middle.

See also 👮‍♀️ Woman Police Officer or 👮‍♂️ Man Police Officer

Police Officer was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
