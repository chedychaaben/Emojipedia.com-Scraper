Emoji Meaning

The Person: Medium-Dark Skin Tone, Curly Hair emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, 🏾 Medium-Dark Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🦱 Curly Hair. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Person: Medium-Dark Skin Tone, Curly Hair was added to Emoji 12.1 in 2019.
