Emoji Meaning

A person with a beard.

This emoji may be used to talk about bearded people in a gender-inclusive way. It was approved in 2017. Gendered variants of this emoji were approved three years later.

Variants include 🧔‍♂️ Man with a Beard and 🧔‍♀️ Woman with a Beard, both approved in 2020.

Person: Beard was approved as part of Unicode 10.0 in 2017 under the name “Bearded Person” and added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
