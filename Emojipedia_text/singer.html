Emoji Meaning

A singer or rock star generally displayed with a microphone, and sometimes musical notes.

iOS and Android display this emoji as a Bowie-esque figure while WhatsApp appears heavily inspired by Prince.

The Singer emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🎤 Microphone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Singer was added to Emoji 12.1 in 2019.
