Emoji Meaning

A magical humanoid creature found in myths and fantasy stories. Generally depicted as being diminutive in size and having insect-inspired wings.

Previously within Windows 11 this Light Skin Tone emoji erroneously displayed the same design as 🧚 Fairy without a skin tone modifier.

The Fairy: Light Skin Tone emoji is a modifier sequence combining 🧚 Fairy and 🏻 Light Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Fairy: Light Skin Tone was added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.
