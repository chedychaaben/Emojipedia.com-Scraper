Emoji Meaning

A classic teapot with a wide bulbous body, a spout, a handle, and a lid. Can be used to talk about tea, tea time, or warm beverages in general.

May also be used to talk about gossip as in the expression spill the tea.

Teapot was approved as part of Unicode 13.0 in 2020 and added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
