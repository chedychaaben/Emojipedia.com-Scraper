Emoji Meaning

The Heart Hands: Light Skin Tone emoji is a modifier sequence combining 🫶 Heart Hands and 🏻 Light Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Heart Hands: Light Skin Tone was added to Emoji 14.0 in 2021.

🚩 Approved in September 2021 as part of Emoji 14.0. Now available across all major emoji platforms.
