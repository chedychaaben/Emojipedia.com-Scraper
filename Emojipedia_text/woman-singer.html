Emoji Meaning

A woman singer or rock star generally displayed with a microphone, and sometimes musical notes.

iOS and Android display this emoji as a Bowie-esque figure while WhatsApp appears heavily inspired by Prince.

The Woman Singer emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👩 Woman, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🎤 Microphone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Woman Singer was added to Emoji 4.0 in 2016.
