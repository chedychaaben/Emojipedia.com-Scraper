Emoji Meaning

The male version of the ⛹ Person With Ball emoji. Currently identical in appearance to the non-gendered base emoji.

The Man Bouncing Ball emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining ⛹️ Person Bouncing Ball, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and ♂️ Male Sign. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Man Bouncing Ball was added to Emoji 4.0 in 2016.
