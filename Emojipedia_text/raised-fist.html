Emoji Meaning

A fist raised in the air that may be used as a celebratory gesture: the fist pump. Can also be used to represent zero items, due to a lack of any fingers being held up.

Sometimes used as a symbol of resistance or defiance. Variations with darker skin tone are commonly used to represent the #BlackLivesMatter movement.

Raised Fist was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
