Emoji Meaning

A two-piece bikini, worn at the beach, pool, or on vacation at a tropical destination.

Shown in a variety of colors and patterns across different platforms.

Related emojis include 🩲 Briefs, 🩳 Shorts, and 🩱 One-Piece Swimsuit.

Bikini was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
