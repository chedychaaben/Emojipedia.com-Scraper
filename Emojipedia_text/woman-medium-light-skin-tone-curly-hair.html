Emoji Meaning

The Woman: Medium-Light Skin Tone, Curly Hair emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👩 Woman, 🏼 Medium-Light Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🦱 Curly Hair. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Woman: Medium-Light Skin Tone, Curly Hair was added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
