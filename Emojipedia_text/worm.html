Emoji Meaning

A pink or brown worm. Most platforms have more realistic designs, though Google and Samsung feature cartoony worms who are smiling sweetly.

Can be used to talk about worms or disease spread by worms. Can also be used to refer to a deceitful person. May be used to in reference to the dance move 'the worm.'

Worm was approved as part of Unicode 13.0 in 2020 and added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
