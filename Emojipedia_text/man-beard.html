Emoji Meaning

A man shown with long hair and a beard.

Note that as of late 2021, Samsung's design displays this emoji with a more gender-neutral design than that used for their 🧔 Person with Beard emoji.

The Man: Beard emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧔 Person: Beard, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and ♂️ Male Sign. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Man: Beard was added to Emoji 13.1 in 2020.
