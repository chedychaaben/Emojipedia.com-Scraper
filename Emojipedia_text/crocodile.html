Emoji Meaning

A crocodile, a stealthy reptile with massive jaws. Generally depicted as a light-green crocodile on all fours, with its long tail curving down to its toothed jaws, facing left.

May resemble and be used for an alligator.

Facebook's crocodile is opening its jaws. Samsung’s design previously resembled a kind of cartoon dinosaur.

Crocodile was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Appearance historically differs greatly cross-platform. Use with caution. 🚨
