Emoji Meaning

The female version of the 🏋 Weight Lifter emoji.

The Woman Lifting Weights emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🏋️ Person Lifting Weights, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and ♀️ Female Sign. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Woman Lifting Weights was added to Emoji 4.0 in 2016.
