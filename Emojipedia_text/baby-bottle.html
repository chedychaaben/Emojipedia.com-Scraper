Emoji Meaning

A bottle of milk or formula, as or fed to a baby, with a bluish cap and rubber nipple or teat. Positioned upright or at a 45° angle, and filled to various levels, sometimes showing fill lines. Often used for newborns and childrearing. See also Breast-Feeding.

Baby Bottle was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
