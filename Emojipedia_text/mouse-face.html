Emoji Meaning

A friendly, cartoon-styled face of a mouse, looking straight ahead. Generally depicted as a gray or white mouse face with large, round ears, a pink, pointed nose, and whiskers.

Often used with a more affectionate tone than the full-bodied 🐁 Mouse. Not to be confused with 🐹 Hamster Face.

Google and Facebook’s designs feature a mouse with buck teeth. Microsoft’s design was previously brown.

Mouse Face was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
