Emoji Meaning

An ox, a bovine used as a draft animal. Depicted in brown in full profile on all fours facing left, with a tail and short horns.

One of the 12 animals of the Chinese zodiac. Often used to represent ♉ Taurus (the Bull) in the Western zodiac. 2021 is Year of the Ox.

Often used to represent a bull and occasionally paired with 💩 Pile of Poo to stand in for slang terms. Not to be confused with 🐃 Water Buffalo.

Ox was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
