Emoji Meaning

An upset person, frowning with dismay. Gender is not specified, but generally shown as a woman.

Previously displayed as a man on Samsung and Microsoft platforms.

Person Frowning was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
