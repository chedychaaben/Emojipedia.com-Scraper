Emoji Meaning

A person going for a walk. Taking a slow-medium pace, this person would be a runner if they picked up their speed.

This emoji does not specify a gender, but is shown as a man on most major platforms.

Person Walking was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Pedestrian” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
