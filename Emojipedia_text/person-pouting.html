Emoji Meaning

A person with a serious expression, showing mild displeasure with the situation. Could be considered to be reserved or stoic in response, as this person is not visible angry.

Quite different to the Pouting Face Emoji which conveys a more aggressive tone, and is a smiley. Pouting Cat Face is a cat.

Person Pouting was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Person with Pouting Face” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
