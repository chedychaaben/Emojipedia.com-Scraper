Emoji Meaning

The Person in Motorized Wheelchair: Medium Skin Tone emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, 🏽 Medium Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🦼 Motorized Wheelchair. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Person in Motorized Wheelchair: Medium Skin Tone was added to Emoji 12.1 in 2019.
