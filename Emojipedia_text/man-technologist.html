Emoji Meaning

A man behind a computer screen, working in the field of technology.

See also: 👩‍💻 Female Technologist

The Man Technologist emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👨 Man, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 💻 Laptop. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Man Technologist was added to Emoji 4.0 in 2016.
