Emoji Meaning

A person with curly hair. This emoji may be used to talk about people with curly hair in a gender-inclusive way.

Variants include 👩‍🦱 Woman with Curly Hair and 👨‍🦱 Man with Curly Hair.

The Person: Curly Hair emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🦱 Curly Hair. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Person: Curly Hair was added to Emoji 12.1 in 2019.
