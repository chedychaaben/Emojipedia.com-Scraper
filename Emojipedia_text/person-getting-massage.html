Emoji Meaning

A person getting his or her face massaged.

Apple’s artwork for this character shows more of a head massage. Google and Microsoft display a more accurate face massage, with hands rubbing the cheeks and face.

Person Getting Massage was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Face Massage” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
