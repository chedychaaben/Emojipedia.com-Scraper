Emoji Meaning

A flamingo, a waterbird with pink feathers and long neck and legs. Depicted as a flamingo in full profile facing left, standing on one leg, usually with a long, black beak.

May be used to represent summer fun or as a pink accent color.

Flamingo was approved as part of Unicode 12.0 in 2019 and added to Emoji 12.0 in 2019.
