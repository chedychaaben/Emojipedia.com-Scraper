Emoji Meaning

Japanese sake, an alcoholic beverage made from rice. Shown as a sake set with a carafe (tokkuri) and drinking cup (choko). Most platforms show a cream colored sake set with a blue or red stripe motif. Samsung's design used to show the Japanese script for 'sake' in red along the side of the carafe.

Sake was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Sake Bottle and Cup” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
