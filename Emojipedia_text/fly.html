Emoji Meaning

A fly with a dark or iridescent green body, six legs, and two translucent wings. On most platforms, its eyes are red.

Can be used to talk about the insect or flight in general. May also be used metaphorically as an adjective meaning "cool."

Fly was approved as part of Unicode 13.0 in 2020 and added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
