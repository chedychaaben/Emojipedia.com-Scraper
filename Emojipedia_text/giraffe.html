Emoji Meaning

A giraffe, the tallest animal on Earth, with spots and a long neck. Generally depicted in yellow or tan in full profile on all fours facing left, with distinctive brown spots and small, horn-like structures.

Samsung’s designs feature a giraffe face looking straight ahead. Facebook’s design previously featured only a giraffe face, as did Microsoft's.

Giraffe was approved as part of Unicode 10.0 in 2017 under the name “Giraffe Face” and added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.
