Emoji Meaning

The Woman and Man Holding Hands: Medium-Light Skin Tone, Dark Skin Tone emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👩 Woman, 🏼 Medium-Light Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner, 🤝 Handshake, 👨 Man and 🏿 Dark Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Woman and Man Holding Hands: Medium-Light Skin Tone, Dark Skin Tone was added to Emoji 12.0 in 2019.
