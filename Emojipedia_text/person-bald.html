Emoji Meaning

A person with a bald head. Can be used to represent a bald person or a person with a shaved head. May also be used to represent a person experiencing hair loss from chemotherapy, alopecia, or other medical treatments or conditions. 

This emoji may be used to talk about bald people in a gender-inclusive way.

Variants include 👩‍🦲 Bald Woman and 👨‍🦲 Bald Man.

The Person: Bald emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🦲 Bald. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Person: Bald was added to Emoji 12.1 in 2019.
