Emoji Meaning

A bed with a gender-unspecified person sleeping in it, as at a house, hotel, hostel, or other sleeping accommodation. Used especially for various content concerning sleeping as well as beds, furniture, housing, relaxing, and other bedroom activities.

Shown with a headboard at the left and footboard with a person under the covers, head on the pillow. Most platforms suggest a wooden frame and feature blue or red sheets.

The appearance of the person varies. Apple and Facebook's designs show only the hair of the sleeper, Apple's black and Facebook's brown and tousled. Other major vendors show the head of the sleeper in default yellow with short, brown or blonde hair. Skin tone support is not recommended for this emoji, though it is supported on some platforms.

Vendors implement the same style of bed as for their 🛏️ Bed, but with a sleeper in it.

Person in Bed was approved as part of Unicode 7.0 in 2014 under the name “Sleeping Accommodation” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
