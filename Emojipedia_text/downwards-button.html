Emoji Meaning

A triangle-shaped arrow, pointing down. Styled similarly to the left and right pointing triangles and colored as either gray or blue, but shown in orange on Android.

Previously displayed as red on Windows.

Downwards Button was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Down-Pointing Small Red Triangle” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
