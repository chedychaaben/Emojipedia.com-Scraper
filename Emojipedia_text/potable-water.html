Emoji Meaning

A symbol for potable (drinkable) water, usually shown with a faucet filling a drinking glass. Commonly used for various content concerning water, drinking, thirst, and dripping.

Some platforms, including Apple and WhatsApp, depict the emoji as an object while others feature a stylized, white faucet on a blue square, as a sign or icon of water that is safe to drink. Compare 🚱 Non-Potable Water, which indicates water that is not safe to drink.

Apple's design previously featured a blue symbol design, Microsoft's a cup filled with water.

Potable Water was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Potable Water Symbol” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
