Emoji Meaning

A mouth with the top teeth biting down on one side of the lower lip.

Used in romantic contexts to express flirtation or arousal. Can be used to express anticipation or excitement in general. Also may be used to express anxiety, pain, worry, or insecurity.

Biting Lip was approved as part of Unicode 14.0 in 2021 and added to Emoji 14.0 in 2021.
