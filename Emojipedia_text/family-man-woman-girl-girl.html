Emoji Meaning

A family with a father, mother, and two daughters.

The Family: Man, Woman, Girl, Girl emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👨 Man, ‍ Zero Width Joiner, 👩 Woman and 👧 Girl. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Family: Man, Woman, Girl, Girl was added to Emoji 2.0 in 2015.
