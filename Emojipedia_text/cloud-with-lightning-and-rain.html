Emoji Meaning

Blue raindrops falling from a white cloud with a lightning bolt flashing.

May be used as a weather icon to represent thundershowers.

Not to be confused with 🌩️ Cloud With Lightning, though their applications may overlap.

Cloud with Lightning and Rain was approved as part of Unicode 5.2 in 2009 under the name “Thunder Cloud and Rain” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
