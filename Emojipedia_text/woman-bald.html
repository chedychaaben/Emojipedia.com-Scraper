Emoji Meaning

A woman with a bald head. Can be used to represent a bald woman or a woman with a shaved head. May also be used to represent a woman experiencing hair loss from chemotherapy, alopecia, or other medical treatments or conditions. 

Variants include 🧑‍🦲 Bald Person and 👨‍🦲 Bald Man.

The Woman: Bald emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👩 Woman, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🦲 Bald. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Woman: Bald was added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
