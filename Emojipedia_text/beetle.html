Emoji Meaning

A green or brown beetle shown with antennae and six legs. Can be used to talk about beetles, bugs, or insects in general.

Not to be confused with 🐞 Lady Beetle, 🪳 Cockroach, 🐛 Bug, 🐜 Ant or other emoji representations of insects.

Beetle was approved as part of Unicode 13.0 in 2020 and added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
