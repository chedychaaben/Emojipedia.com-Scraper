Emoji Meaning

A person with arms above their head, making an ‘OK’ sign (circle) with the whole body. No gender is specified.

Previously displayed as an alien or blob on Android, a man on Windows, and a woman on most other platforms.

Person Gesturing OK was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Face with OK Gesture” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
