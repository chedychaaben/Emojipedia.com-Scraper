Emoji Meaning

A desktop personal computer. Generally depicted as a black or gray monitor with a dark or blue screen.

Like 💻 Laptop Computer, used for various content concerning computer technology, work, and activity. May also be used to represent television, as it resembles a flatscreen TV.

Google and WhatsApp include keyboards. Apple’s design resembles an iMac. Microsoft and Samsung’s designs previously featured PC towers.

Desktop Computer was approved as part of Unicode 7.0 in 2014 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
