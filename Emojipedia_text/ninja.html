Emoji Meaning

A person dressed as a ninja with a black hood, a mask covering the lower half of their face, and the hilt of a knife or sword emerging from behind their shoulder.

The now-discontinued Microsoft ZWJ sequence 🐱‍👤 Ninja Cat did not include the ninja emoji at all as it was developed several years before ninja was approved. It combined 🐱 Cat Face and 👤 Bust in Silhouette and was only available from Microsoft prior to the debut of the Fluent emoji set in November 2021.

Ninja was approved as part of Unicode 13.0 in 2020 and added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
