Emoji Meaning

An older adult with no gender specified. This emoji may be used to talk about older adults in a gender-inclusive way.

Variants include 👴 Older Man or 👵 Older Woman. See also 🧑‍🦳 Person with White Hair, which is similar, though is shown with white hair and without wrinkles on most platforms.

Older Person was approved as part of Unicode 10.0 in 2017 under the name “Older Adult” and added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.
