Emoji Meaning

A club symbol emoji, which is used in card games for the clubs suit.

Apple's design previously displayed the black spade symbol on a white playing card.

See the glossary for information on the term black in unicode character names.

Club Suit was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Black Club Suit” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
