Emoji Meaning

A kiwifruit, or Chinese gooseberry, with its fuzzy, brown skin sliced open to show its light-green flesh, white core, and black seeds. 

May be associated with New Zealand, whose residents are nicknamed Kiwis, after the bird.

Kiwi Fruit was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 under the name “Kiwifruit” and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
