Emoji Meaning

A person in traditional western wedding dress with a veil over their head.

Person with Veil was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Bride with Veil” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance. Platforms switched to a gender-inclusive design for this emoji in 2020.
