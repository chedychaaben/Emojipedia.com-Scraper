Emoji Meaning

A face showing a spiral eyes, commonly used to indicate dizziness, but may also represent hypnosis or other forms of disorientation.

Created to disambiguate an existing emoji which previously displayed spiral eyes on some platforms, but X eyes on others. This emoji will consistently show spiral eyes on all platforms.

The Face with Spiral Eyes emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 😵 Face with Crossed-Out Eyes, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 💫 Dizzy. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Face with Spiral Eyes was added to Emoji 13.1 in 2020.
