Emoji Meaning

A person with a ball, which is generally displayed as a basketballer running and dribbling (bouncing) the basketball.

Person Bouncing Ball was approved as part of Unicode 5.2 in 2009 under the name “Person with Ball” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
