Emoji Meaning

A thick check mark / tick displayed on some platforms in a handwritten style. Generally displayed in black. Commonly used in lists to signify completed tasks or verification of a statement of fact.

Previously shown in red on Samsung and green on Google and Microsoft. Note: this emoji is not supported in Twitter names, to minimize confusion with a verified checkmark.

See also: ✅ Check Mark Button, ☑️ Check Box with Check.

Check Mark was approved as part of Unicode 1.1 in 1993 under the name “Heavy Check Mark” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
