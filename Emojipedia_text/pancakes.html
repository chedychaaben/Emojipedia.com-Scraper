Emoji Meaning

A stack of breakfast pancakes, topped with a pad of butter and maple syrup oozing down its golden-brown sides, onto a plate on some platforms.Â 

Especially popular on Pancake Tuesday.

An alternative use for this emoji on TikTok and other platforms: to represent that someone has a flat bottom ('flat as a pancake').

Pancakes was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
