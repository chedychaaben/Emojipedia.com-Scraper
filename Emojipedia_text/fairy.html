Emoji Meaning

A magical humanoid creature found in myths and fantasy stories. Generally depicted as being diminutive in size and having insect-inspired wings.

This emoji supports skin tones, and variations that are female or male.

Fairy was approved as part of Unicode 10.0 in 2017 and added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.
