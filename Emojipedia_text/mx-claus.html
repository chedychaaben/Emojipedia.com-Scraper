Emoji Meaning

A person, generally depicted as being older with gray hair, wearing a red Christmas (aka "Santa") hat. Does not represent any specific character in folklore. Mx Claus provides a gender inclusive alternative to 🎅 Santa Claus or 🤶 Mrs. Claus.

Apple depicts Mx Claus as wearing glasses, while other vendors do not.

Mx is an honorific that does not indicate gender.

The Mx Claus emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🎄 Christmas Tree. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Mx Claus was added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
