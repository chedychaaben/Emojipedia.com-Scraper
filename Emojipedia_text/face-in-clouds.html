Emoji Meaning

A face that appears surrounded by clouds or a haze of smoke. Ambiguous in appearance, this may represent a foggy state of mind, confusion, or even a sense of calm bliss.

May also be used to indicate the presence of smoke.

The Face in Clouds emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 😶 Face Without Mouth, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🌫️ Fog. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Face in Clouds was added to Emoji 13.1 in 2020.
