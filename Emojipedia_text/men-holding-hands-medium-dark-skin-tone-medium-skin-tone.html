Emoji Meaning

The Men Holding Hands: Medium-Dark Skin Tone, Medium Skin Tone emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 👨 Man, 🏾 Medium-Dark Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner, 🤝 Handshake and 🏽 Medium Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Men Holding Hands: Medium-Dark Skin Tone, Medium Skin Tone was added to Emoji 12.0 in 2019.
