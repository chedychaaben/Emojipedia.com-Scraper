Emoji Meaning

A couple standing next to each other, smiling, with a love heart floating in the air between them. No gender is specified for the two people, and as such this may be used to represent the concept of a couple broadly, and/or as a means of showing two non-binary people.

Previously shown with the same appearance as 👩‍❤️‍👨 Couple With Heart: Woman, Man, but now displays two people with an ambiguous gender appearance.

Couple with Heart was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
