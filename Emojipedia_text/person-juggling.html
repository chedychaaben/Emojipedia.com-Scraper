Emoji Meaning

A person juggling between three and five colored balls. The person shown in this emoji wears a hat on most major platforms.

Early Twitter and Facebook designs previously displayed this emoji as a pair of disembodied hands juggling three colored balls.

Person Juggling was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 under the name “Juggling” and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
