Emoji Meaning

A swan, a waterbird with white feathers and a long neck. Depicted in full profile facing left, with a curving neck, orange bill, black eye marking, and wings and legs tucked in, as if gliding in water.

May be used to represent a goose or, metaphorically, love, grace, and beauty.

Swan was approved as part of Unicode 11.0 in 2018 and added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
