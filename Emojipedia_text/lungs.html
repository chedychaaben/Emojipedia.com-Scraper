Emoji Meaning

Pink anatomical lungs and trachea, shown with bronchial tubes on some platforms. Used in medical contexts to talk about lung health. Sometimes used in reference to taking deep breaths, hyperventilating, or breathing in general. Also used in the context of smoking or vaping.

Lungs was approved as part of Unicode 13.0 in 2020 and added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
