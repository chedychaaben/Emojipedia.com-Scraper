Emoji Meaning

Two pink love hearts. One larger than the other. Can be used to display that “love is in the air”.

On Snapchat, this emoji next to a contact denotes that you have been #1 BFs with each other for two consecutive months. A bug in October 2019 has a yellow heart emoji showing instead of 💕 for some Snapchat users.

Two Hearts was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
