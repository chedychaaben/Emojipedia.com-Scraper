Emoji Meaning

The flag for Wales, a country in the United Kingdom. May show as the letters gbwls.

The Flag: Wales emoji is a tag sequence combining 🏴 Black Flag, 󠁧 Tag Latin Small Letter G, 󠁢 Tag Latin Small Letter B, 󠁷 Tag Latin Small Letter W, 󠁬 Tag Latin Small Letter L, 󠁳 Tag Latin Small Letter S and 󠁿 Cancel Tag. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Flag: Wales was added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.
