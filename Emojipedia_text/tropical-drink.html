Emoji Meaning

A sunset-orange-colored tropical beverage in a stemmed glass, as a rum punch or mai tai. Served with a straw and fruit garnish. Many platforms include a cocktail umbrella, hence umbrella drink. Samsung previously featured an electric-blue concoction. See also Cocktail Glass.

Tropical Drink was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
