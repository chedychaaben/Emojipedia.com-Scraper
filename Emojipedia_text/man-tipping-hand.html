Emoji Meaning

A man tipping his hand by his shoulder as if he’s carrying a tray of drinks or flipping his hair.

This emoji is commonly used to express sassiness or sarcasm.

The Man Tipping Hand emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 💁 Person Tipping Hand, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and ♂️ Male Sign. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Man Tipping Hand was added to Emoji 4.0 in 2016.
