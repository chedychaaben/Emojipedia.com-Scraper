Emoji Meaning

The Pregnant Person: Medium-Dark Skin Tone emoji is a modifier sequence combining 🫄 Pregnant Person and 🏾 Medium-Dark Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Pregnant Person: Medium-Dark Skin Tone was added to Emoji 14.0 in 2021.
