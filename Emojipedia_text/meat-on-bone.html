Emoji Meaning

A hunk of unspecified meat on a white bone, as associated with cartoon cavepersons or food in anime and manga (Japanese ano niku, “that meat”).

This emoji was shown as a steak in Android 4.4.

Meat on Bone was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
