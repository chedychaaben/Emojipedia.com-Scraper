Emoji Meaning

Two or more silver paper clips linked together, generally forming an inverted V. Microsoft’s design displays three paper clips.

Occasionally used to represent school/office supplies or metaphorical connections. Also sometimes used as an icon before a hyperlink.

Not to be confused with 🔗 Link or ⛓️ Chains.

Linked Paperclips was approved as part of Unicode 7.0 in 2014 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
