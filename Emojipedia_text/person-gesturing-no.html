Emoji Meaning

A person with arms crossed forming an ‘X’ to indicate ‘no’ or ‘no good’. This motion is used on the TV game show Deal or No Deal to indicate ‘No Deal’.

Person Gesturing No was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Face with No Good Gesture” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
