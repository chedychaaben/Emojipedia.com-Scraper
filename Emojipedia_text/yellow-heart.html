Emoji Meaning

A yellow or gold colored heart, frequently used alongside other colored hearts. This displayed as a pink hairy heart on Android 4.4.

On Snapchat, this emoji next to a contact denotes a person that you snap the most, and who also snaps you the most (#1 best friend). The yellow #1 BF heart turns into a red heart after two weeks.

Yellow Heart was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
