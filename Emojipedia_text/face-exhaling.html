Emoji Meaning

A face showing a visible breath of air being dispelled. Used to represent relief, exhaustion, or as a sigh of disappointment or displeasure.

May also be used to indicate the exhaling of smoke.

The Face Exhaling emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 😮 Face with Open Mouth, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 💨 Dashing Away. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Face Exhaling was added to Emoji 13.1 in 2020.
