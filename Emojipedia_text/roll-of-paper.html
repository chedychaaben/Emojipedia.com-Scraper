Emoji Meaning

A roll of paper, as toilet paper (toilet tissue). May also be a roll of kitchen/paper towels. Depicted at various angles, with a sheet unfurling.

Commonly used for toilet paper but may also be used for various content concerning toilets, going to the bathroom, bathrooms, cleaning, or paper more generally.

Roll of Paper was approved as part of Unicode 11.0 in 2018 and added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
