Emoji Meaning

A snail, a slow-moving, snail-like mollusk in a spiraling shell. Generally depicted as a brown snail in full profile facing left or right, with a brown, textured shell and tentacle-like eyes.

May be used to represent a slug and various senses of slow.

Twitter’s snail has a purple body and yellow shell. Microsoft’s snail previously had a green body.

Snail was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
