Emoji Meaning

The Person with Veil: Medium Skin Tone emoji is a modifier sequence combining 👰 Person with Veil and 🏽 Medium Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Person with Veil: Medium Skin Tone was added to Emoji 2.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance. Platforms switched to a gender-inclusive design for this emoji in 2020.
