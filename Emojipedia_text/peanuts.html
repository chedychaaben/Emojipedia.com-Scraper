Emoji Meaning

The pod of the peanut, shown in its rounded, grooved, light-brown shell. Apple depicts a pair.

May be used for peanut butter as well as other edible nuts (e.g., almonds) or metaphorical ones, including slang for “crazy”, “testicle”, or to nut (ejaculate).

Peanuts was approved as part of Unicode 9.0 in 2016 and added to Emoji 3.0 in 2016.
