Emoji Meaning

A tall boot generally featuring a slight heel, shown in brown or red on most platforms.

Some platforms previously display this emoji as two boots (e.g. Google, Samsung), while today most display a single boot.

Woman’s Boot was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Womans Boots” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
