Emoji Meaning

The snout of a pig. Depicted as a pink oval or slightly pear-shaped nose with two black nostrils.

See also 🐖 Pig and 🐷 Pig Face.

Previously displayed as a cute pig character on Google Gmail.

Pig Nose was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
