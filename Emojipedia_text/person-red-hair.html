Emoji Meaning

A person with red hair. This emoji maintains red hair no matter which skin tone is chosen. This emoji may be used to talk about redheads in a gender-inclusive way.

Variants include 👩‍🦰 Woman with Red Hair and 👨‍🦰 Man with Red Hair.

The Person: Red Hair emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and 🦰 Red Hair. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Person: Red Hair was added to Emoji 12.1 in 2019.
