Emoji Meaning

A cup of a steaming-hot beverage, usually taken as coffee or tea. Sometimes shown on a saucer.

Samsung's design previously featured a foamy-white tulip latte art design.

May be paired with the Frog Face to represent the snarky But That's None of My Business meme. 

Hot Beverage was approved as part of Unicode 4.0 in 2003 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
