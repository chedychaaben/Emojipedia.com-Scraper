Emoji Meaning

A yellow sun half covered by a white cloud. Position of the sun varies across platforms.

May be used as a weather icon to represent a partly cloudy day.

Not to be confused with 🌤️ Sun Behind Small Cloud or ⛅ Sun Behind Large Cloud, though their applications may overlap.

Microsoft’s cloud was previously gray, Samsung’s bluish.

Sun Behind Cloud was approved as part of Unicode 5.2 in 2009 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
