Emoji Meaning

The Couple with Heart: Person, Person, Dark Skin Tone, Medium-Dark Skin Tone emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🧑 Person, 🏿 Dark Skin Tone, ‍ Zero Width Joiner, ❤️ Red Heart and 🏾 Medium-Dark Skin Tone. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Couple with Heart: Person, Person, Dark Skin Tone, Medium-Dark Skin Tone was added to Emoji 13.1 in 2020.
