Emoji Meaning

A friendly, cartoon-styled face of polar bear, looking straight ahead. Depicted as the face of a white bear with round ears.

WhatsApp previously showed the full body of the bear.

The Polar Bear emoji is a ZWJ sequence combining 🐻 Bear, ‍ Zero Width Joiner and ❄️ Snowflake. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Polar Bear was added to Emoji 13.0 in 2020.
