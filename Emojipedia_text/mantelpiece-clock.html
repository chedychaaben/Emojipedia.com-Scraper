Emoji Meaning

An antique-styled mantel clock with a flat base and rounded case, as made of wood and brass and placed above a fireplace.

Commonly used for various content concerning time. May also suggest a traditional home or old-time environment. 

Samsung’s design previously featured a more modern-looking, blue clock.

Mantelpiece Clock was approved as part of Unicode 7.0 in 2014 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
