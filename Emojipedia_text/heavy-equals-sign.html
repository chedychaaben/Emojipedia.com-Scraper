Emoji Meaning

An equals sign in the style of the already existing set of mathematical symbols ✖️ Multiply, ➕ Plus, ➖ Minus, and ➗ Divide.

Heavy Equals Sign was approved as part of Unicode 14.0 in 2021 and added to Emoji 14.0 in 2021.

🚩 Approved in September 2021 as part of Emoji 14.0. Now available across all major emoji platforms.
