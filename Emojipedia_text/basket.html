Emoji Meaning

A basket, as used for storing or carrying such things as food or clothes. Appearance widely varies across platforms but generally depicted as woven from wicker with one or more handles. Commonly used to represent doing laundry or having a picnic.

Apple and WhatsApp’s designs, featuring a white cloth, suggest a bread or picnic basket. Samsung’s design displays green fabric, as knitted from yarn.

Microsoft’s design previously suggested a laundry basket by holding a blue garment or cloth.

Not to be confused with 🗑️ Wastebasket.

Basket was approved as part of Unicode 11.0 in 2018 and added to Emoji 11.0 in 2018.
