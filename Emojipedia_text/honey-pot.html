Emoji Meaning

A golden-yellow pot of honey, as made by bees and desired by bears. Shown with the sweet substance oozing down its sides, often with a honey dipper inside the small vessel. 

May be used figuratively, as the term of endearment “honey.”

Honey Pot was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
