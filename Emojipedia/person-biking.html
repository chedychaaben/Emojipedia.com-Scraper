🚴 Person Biking

Emoji Meaning

A person riding a bicycle for leisure, sport or transport. Intended to be gender-neutral.

Person Biking was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 under the name “Bicyclist” and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.

🚩 Previously displayed a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.
