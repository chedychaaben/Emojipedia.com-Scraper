🇬🇳 Flag: Guinea

Emoji Meaning

The flag for Guinea, which may show as the letters GN on some platforms.

The Flag: Guinea emoji is a flag sequence combining 🇬 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter G and 🇳 Regional Indicator Symbol Letter N. These display as a single emoji on supported platforms.

Flag: Guinea was added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
